My Work

BODA Lens LLC

Illustrator | Photoshop | Wix | Alibaba
Overview

Few things are more exciting than starting your own business. It is stressful, exhausting and requires tremendous faith that things will work out. When my brother in-law came to me with the idea of creating our own camera lenses, I was a bit apprehensive at first. I was recently married, in school full-time and had no money. I could not afford the sweat equity required to make the company successful. However, I knew it would be an invaluable experience and would push me to do things I didn't think possible. My wonderful wife was a major advocate of taking the risk. And I am so grateful she was. Being able to design a product and see it through the whole product development life cycyle taught me more than anything I was studying in school or working on at my 9-5 job.

The Process

My brother in-law and I began by brainstorming the product. We had done significant research on current amazon trends and knew we wanted to do mobile phone accessories. Once we decided on camera lenses we began reaching out to manufacturers. We began testing products from different factories until we found the material we wanted. I then worked closely with the chosen manufacturer to make changes and adjustment to the product until it was exactly what we wanted. Not only was I in charge of the product design, I design all packaging and promotional material including, our website, facebook ads, user manuals, in-store displays and trade show displays. The design process was a blast. We were able to get a few famous instagrammers to be our beta testers. They provided extremely valuable feedback on the product and our designs. I continued to make changes and adapt to the feedback that was received.

The Finished Product

Once the first full shipment arrived we went to work. We sold everywhere we could. We built our online presence and began attending tradeshows. We then began reaching out to local stores. After lots of product demos we were able to land a deal with one of Utah's biggest mobile phone repair stores, Bad Apple. Within a week we had our product in 15 stores across the state fo Utah. Then within just a couple of months we were able to earn back our initial investment and get in the green.
