My Work

The Oil Barn

Photoshop | Illustrator | Sketch | Wix
Overview

Farmers really are the heart of this country. Having a chance to help a young man bring his brick and mortar business online was a real treat. The Oil Barn needed a scalable and easy to maintain ecommerce site. And they needed it quick. I designed their new site to establish a brand identity that exemplifies the love of the earth and of hard work that these farmers possess.

The Process

As with many of my projects, time is of the essence. To solve this problem I went with the WiX platform for ease of use. The client also wanted to self-maintain the site in the future which made Wix a good, simple choice. Choosing the colors was particularly hard as they played a vital role in establishing the feel of the brand and company. I was able to create a palette that the CEO loved.

The Finished Product
